DEVTrails 2026 – Phase 1 Submission
AI-Powered Income Stabilizer Wallet for Gig Delivery Workers

1. Introduction
In today’s fast-growing gig economy, delivery workers play a crucial role in ensuring smooth urban services. However, their income is highly unpredictable as it depends on daily working conditions. External factors such as heavy rainfall, extreme temperatures, traffic congestion, or sudden restrictions can significantly reduce their ability to work and earn.
Unlike traditional insurance systems that compensate after a loss, this solution introduces a proactive approach to income management. We propose an AI-Powered Income Stabilizer Wallet, a system that maintains financial stability by balancing earnings across good and bad days.
Instead of providing one-time payouts, the system intelligently saves a small portion of earnings during high-income days and supports the user during low-income days. This ensures consistent income flow, reduced financial stress, and better planning.

2. User Persona – Arjun
Arjun Reddy is a 24-year-old delivery partner working in Hyderabad for platforms like Swiggy and Zomato. Originally from a small town, he moved to the city to support his parents and fund his younger brother’s education.
Arjun completes around 100–120 deliveries per week. His earnings vary significantly depending on demand and external conditions. On some days, he earns well, but during heavy rain or extreme heat, his working hours reduce, leading to lower income.
Each week, he sends approximately ₹7,000–₹9,000 to his family. Even a few low-income days can disturb his financial stability. Arjun needs a simple and automatic system that ensures consistent daily earnings without complex processes.

3. User Goals
Arjun’s main goals include:
•	Maintaining a stable weekly income
•	Supporting his family consistently
•	Reducing financial uncertainty due to external factors
•	Avoiding stress caused by fluctuating earnings
•	Using a simple and automated financial support system

4. User Needs
To effectively support users like Arjun, the system must provide:
•	Automatic savings during high-income days
•	Financial support during low-income days
•	A simple and transparent wallet system
•	Minimal user effort (fully automated process)
•	Smart insights for better earning decisions

5. Problem Statement
Gig delivery workers frequently face income instability due to uncontrollable external factors such as weather, traffic, or fluctuating demand. Existing solutions mainly focus on compensation after losses and often involve delays or complex processes.
The challenge is to design a real-time, automated, and intelligent system that stabilizes income by balancing earnings over time, ensuring workers do not face financial difficulties during low-income periods.

6. Key Use Cases
Case 1: High-Earning Day
On a busy day, Arjun earns ₹1200.
•	System automatically saves a small portion (₹100–₹150) into the wallet
•	Remaining amount is available for daily use
 Outcome: Savings are built effortlessly

Case 2: Low-Earning Day (Rainfall)
Due to heavy rain, Arjun earns only ₹500.
•	System detects below-average income
•	₹300 is automatically added from wallet balance
 Outcome: Daily income becomes stable

Case 3: Demand Drop
Low order demand reduces earnings.
•	System identifies reduced income
•	Partial support is provided from wallet
Outcome: Financial stress is minimized

Case 4: Predicted Risk Day
System predicts possible disruption (e.g., rain next day).
•	User is notified in advance
•	Suggestion to save more from current earnings
 Outcome: Better financial preparedness

7. User Journey
Stage 1: Activation
User activates the Income Stabilizer Wallet.
•	System sets saving rules automatically
Stage 2: Daily Work
User continues deliveries as usual.
•	System tracks earnings in the background
Stage 3: Smart Saving
On high-income days, small amounts are saved automatically
Stage 4: Income Support
On low-income days, wallet balance is used to stabilize earnings
Stage 5: Insights & Alerts
System provides predictions and financial suggestions
